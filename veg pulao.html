Veg Pulao - The Taste of India

Last updated: 10/01/2025

Pulao, also known as rice pilaf, is comfort food at its best. This easy one-pot, mildly flavored, and fragrant vegetarian dish is packed with spiced rice and vegetables. It pairs well with raita, pickle, and roasted papad.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, etc.)
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Wash the basmati rice 2-3 times and soak it in water for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, and cumin seeds.
  3. Once the cumin splutters, add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and green chili, and sauté for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
  6. Add soaked rice and mix gently.
  7. Pour in 2 cups of water, bring to a boil, then cover and simmer for 15 minutes on low heat.
  8. Once done, fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with fresh coriander.
  9. Serve hot with raita, pickle, or papad!
