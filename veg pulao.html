Veg pulao - The taste of India.

Last updated: 10/01/2025

Pulao, also known as rice pilaf, is comfort food at its best. I make this Veg Pulao recipe when I want to cook something quick, easy, and satisfying. This easy one-pot, mildly flavored, and fragrant vegetarian dish is bursting with deliciously spiced rice and vegetables. It pairs great with raita, pickle, and roasted papad.
