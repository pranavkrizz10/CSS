Metropolitan Museum of Art

The Metropolitan Museum of Art, also known as "The Met," is one of the largest and most prestigious art museums in the world. Located in New York City, it was founded in 1870 and houses over two million works of art spanning 5,000 years of history.

The museum features collections from ancient civilizations, European masters, and modern artists. It is a cultural hub, attracting millions of visitors each year and hosting renowned exhibitions and events.
