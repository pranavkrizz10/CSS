WELCOME TO NEW YORK

The land that never sleeps.


Location:

  • NEW YORK is located in the state of New York, USA
  • It's situated on the East Coast, along the Atlantic Ocean
  • The city is made up of five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island

History:

  • NEW YORK was originally inhabited by Indigenous peoples, including the Lenape
  • It was founded as New Amsterdam by the Dutch in 1624 and later renamed by the British in 1664
  • NEW YORK played a major role in the American Revolution and became the first U.S. capital in 1789
  • It has been a gateway for millions of immigrants arriving through Ellis Island
