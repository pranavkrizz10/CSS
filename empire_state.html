Empire State Building

The Empire State Building, one of the most iconic skyscrapers in the world, stands at 1,454 feet including its antenna. Located in Midtown Manhattan, it was the tallest building in the world upon its completion in 1931.

The building is an architectural marvel, featuring Art Deco design and offering breathtaking views from its observation decks. It has been a symbol of ambition, resilience, and innovation throughout history.
