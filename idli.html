Idli Recipe - Soft & Fluffy South Indian Delight

Last updated: 10/01/2025

Idli is a soft, fluffy, and healthy South Indian dish made from fermented rice and urad dal (black gram). It is steamed instead of fried, making it light on the stomach and easy to digest. Idlis are often served with coconut chutney, sambar (a lentil-based vegetable stew), or spicy podi (a dry chutney powder). This dish is packed with probiotics due to the fermentation process, which improves gut health and digestion.
